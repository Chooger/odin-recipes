Chicken Bacon Ranch pizza

I mean you can't go wrong with pizza. But what's better than pizza? Pizza with bacon on it. What's better than pizza with bacon on it? Pizza with bacon AND ranch on it. And what's better than pizza with bacon and ranch on it? Pizza with bacon, chicken, and ranch!

    Ingredients

  • Chicken: any kind, about 2 chicken breasts amount
  • Bacon
  • Ranch: any brand or try homemade
  • Pizza dough: any store bought or try homemade

    Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F.
  2. Roll out dough to about a 13-15 inch circle. Place on pizza pan.
  3. Top dough with ranch and spread to an even layer. Sprinkle with cheese. Top with chicken and bacon. Bake for 15 minutes or until crust is golden.
