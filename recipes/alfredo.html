Chicken Alfredo

This is the recipe my girlfriend makes <3

Creamy alfredo sauce on top of noodles, and chicken with a spicy, spanish twist.

    Ingredients

  • 1-2 Chicken breasts
  • 1 box of noodles (fettuccine or cooks choice)
  • 1 jar of alfredo sauce
  • Garlic/onion powder (cooks choice)
  • Salt/pepper
  • Saz√≥n Goya (1 packet)
  • Adobo seasoning
  • Chicken and tomato bouillon powder

    Directions

  1. Marinate the chicken overnight. This step is optional, but preferred. Use the seasonings found above, and mix with water however you you would like it.
  2. Cook the noodles. Bring the water to a boil, and add extra seasoning to the water by taste. Add noodles and cook until done, strain when finished.
  3. Cook the chicken. Cut chicken into chunks of desired size, and fry them on stovetop/really however you'd like to cook them.
  4. Assemble the dish: After everything is cooked, now all that's left is to stir the meat and the alfredo sauce into the noodles
  5. Enjoy :)
