Mushroom Gnochi

I am just a sucker for asian food or pasta really :3

Japanese inspred creamy mushroom nochi with a garlicy, dashi, soy, parmesan and mushroom sauce.

    Ingredients

  • 250 g gnocchi, fresh or frozen
  • 100 g shiitake mushrooms, fresh and sliced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1.5 tbsp oil
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp dashi powder
  • 3 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 3 tbsp grated parmesan cheese

    Directions

  1. Cook the gnocchi according to the package directions. Once cooked, drain and set aside.
  2. Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add the butter and oil, then stir in the sliced shiitake mushrooms. Sauté for 1–2 minutes until they begin to soften.
  3. Add the chopped onion to the pan. Cook for another 3–4 minutes until the onion turns translucent and fragrant.
  4. Stir in the soy sauce and black pepper, mixing well. Let everything cook together for about 30 seconds so the flavors blend.
  5. Pour in the cream and sprinkle in the dashi powder (start with ½ tsp, taste, and adjust if needed). Stir to combine and let the sauce simmer for 2–3 minutes until slightly thickened.
  6. Add the cooked gnocchi into the pan and gently toss to coat in the sauce.
  7. Mix in 2 tbsp of chopped fresh parsley and 2 tablespoons of grated Parmesan cheese, stirring until it melts into the sauce
  8. Serve warm, topped with the remaining 1 tablespoon of parsley and 1 tablespoon of cheese.
